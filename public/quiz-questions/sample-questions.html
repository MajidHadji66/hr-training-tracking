Sample Quiz Questions for HR Training

What is the purpose of the HR Training Tracker system?
To help HR departments manage and track employee training programs, including courses, employees, and departments.
What technologies are used in the frontend of this application?
Angular 20, TypeScript, SCSS, and Angular Material
What is the backend technology stack?
Node.js with Express.js
What database system is planned for this application?
MySQL / Cloud SQL
What is the purpose of employee training tracking?
To monitor required courses, track completion progress, and ensure employees meet their professional development requirements.
How do positions relate to courses in the system?
Positions can have required courses assigned to them, so all employees in that position will need to complete those courses.
What is a completion percentage?
The percentage of required courses that an employee has completed out of their total assigned courses.
What are the two main user views in the application?
Admin Dashboard (for managing all employees and training) and Employee Portal (for viewing personal training)
Why is continuous employee training important?
It ensures employees stay current with industry standards, improves job performance, increases engagement, and helps organizations remain competitive.
What role does training tracking play in compliance?
It provides documented proof that employees have completed required certifications and training, which is essential for regulatory compliance and audits.
